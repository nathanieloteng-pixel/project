What is the Solar System?

Our solar system is made up of the Sun and everything that travels around it.

The Sun is the star at the center of our solar system.

Written by your teacher.

Subscript and Superscript in Science

Water is written as H2O. The number 2 is in subscript.

Carbon dioxide is written as CO2. Another subscript example.

In maths, we write squared as x2. The 2 is in superscript.

A very large number looks like this: 106. That means one million.
